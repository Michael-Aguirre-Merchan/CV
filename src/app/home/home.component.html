I am passionate about video games and more about strategy. That's why I decided to use my knowledge in software development to create a game (obviously, this is a work in progress, there are always things that can be improved and perfected).

Why chess? I think this game has qualities that help you forget the stress of hectic everyday life and be an admirable activity that combines mathematical precision with intuition. If you've played chess before, you know that the most challenging thing is maintaining constant concentration. On the contrary, it is your first time; I could summarize that chess is a game for two players on a board with 32 pieces (16 for each player) of six types. Each piece moves differently. The game's object is to checkmate, that is, to threaten the opponent's king with capture until it is unavoidable.

I hope I can capture the attention of everyone who visits this website and helps a little to create a distraction from the daily routine.
